1. Context: When the intranet becomes obsolete

⚠️Problem

The existing enterprise platform did not meet the modern needs of employees - disparate tools, lack of social interaction, outdated interface.

🎯Goal

Create a unified ecosystem that will combine administrative functions, social tools, a development system and gamification. Turn a mandatory tool into a platform that employees use voluntarily.

What needed to be combined

🔐
Administrative functions

Accesses, management

💬
Social tools

Messenger, events, groups

📈
Development system

Growth plans, knowledge library

🏆
Gamification

Coins, awards, ratings

2. Research: Understanding real-life work scenarios

🔬Methods

15 in-depth interviews Company-wide surveys Usability testing Corridor studies

💡Key insights

👥

🎯
Recognition as a motivator

🧩

📱
Mobility

⚖️

🎮

Coins and ratings went where they were tied to real actions and transparent accrual logic
🔍

15 in-depth interviews and surveys about the company cut off “beautiful for the sake of beautiful” and recorded priorities: sociality, mobility, single sign-on

3. Innovation in the process: Design grooming

🚫Problem

The classic review process created bureaucracy - each small bug turned into a separate task, slowing down the team's work.

✨Solution

Introduced design grooming - monthly one-hour sessions with developers, where up to 10 visual problems were corrected in real time.

80%
Minor UI problems were resolved immediately
↓
The volume of tasks in the backlog has decreased
🗣️
Designers and developers speak the same language

Changing the culture of collaboration between design and development
